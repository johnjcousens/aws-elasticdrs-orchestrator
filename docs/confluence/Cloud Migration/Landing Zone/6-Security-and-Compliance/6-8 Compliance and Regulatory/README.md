### **Background:** Due to the customer’s role as a Covered Entity or Business Associate, the environment must meet the requirements of:

* HIPAA Security Rule (45 CFR §164.3XX)
* HITRUST CSF v11.3 R2 (r2 validated level)
* Privacy- CCPA and Legal requirements- Documented in Attachments and References
